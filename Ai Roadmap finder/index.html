Our Mission

Democratising career clarity for every student in India

CareerAI was built on one belief: every student — regardless of college or city — deserves a clear, personalised career roadmap. We combine curated expert knowledge with AI to deliver step-by-step guidance across 50+ career paths.

  • 🚀 Free forever — no paywalls, no sign-ups required
  • 🇮🇳 India-first — built around Indian exams, boards & industry
  • 🤖 AI-powered — smart portfolio analysis & career matching
  • 🔗 Real mentors — connect with professionals who've walked the path
  • 🗺️ 50+ roadmaps — from ML to Medicine, UPSC to UI/UX
⚙️

How It Works

Four simple steps to your perfect career path

1
Search Any Career

Type a career, skill, or keyword in the search bar. Our fuzzy-matching engine finds the right roadmap instantly from 50+ options.

2
Get Your Roadmap

See a phase-by-phase learning path — from foundation to expert — with curated resources, tools, and milestones at every step.

3
Paste Your Portfolio

Drop your bio or resume snippet into the AI analyzer. It reads your skills and recommends the careers that best match your background.

4
Follow Real Professionals

Every roadmap surfaces verified industry leaders in that field. Connect on LinkedIn for mentorship and networking.

Privacy Policy

Effective date: February 2025

CareerAI is built with your privacy as a first principle. Here's exactly what we do and don't do:

  • ✅ No account required — we never collect your name, email, or personal data
  • ✅ No data stored — portfolio text is analysed entirely in-browser, never sent to any server
  • ✅ No trackers — we don't use advertising SDKs or sell your data to third parties
  • ✅ LinkedIn links — clicking profiles opens LinkedIn in a new tab; their privacy policy applies
  • ✅ Local storage only — used solely to remember lightweight preferences like session state
  • ✅ AI calls — if Gemini AI is enabled, your prompt is sent to Google's Gemini API; no other data is attached
